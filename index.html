The Philadelphia Native

As people from other cities move to Philly, it is important to acknowledge the uniqueness of our city. "The City of Brotherly Love" has its own swag that makes it one of a kind. This article is an ode to our great city. Check this jawn out.

The Most Passionate Fans

Philadelphians will never, I mean never, stop celebrating winning SuperBowl 52 in 2018. Seas of people crowded the streets; climbing street lights, swinging from traffic lights, chanting "E-A-G-L-E-S" nonstop. After this the "Philly Special" became a well known play in the NFL. Let's not forget the time Philadelphia Sixers' fans booed Beyonce's girl group Destiny's Child at the 2001 NBA Finals..

Star Studded

Many of the nation's stars are from Philadelphia. One of the greatest basketball players of all time, Kobe Bryant was from Philly (R.I.P). Not to mention Bab Saget(R.I.P), Kevin Hart, Jill Scott, QuestLove, Tina Fey, Diplo, and Bradley Cooper. Oh by the way, M.Knight Shyamalan is filming his new movie "Knock at the Cabin" in Philly right now.

The Birthplace of America

Philadelphia has the nation's first medical school,university, zoo, art museum, U.S Mint,public library, and the Liberty Bell. Many people do not know, Philadelphia was home to the world's first eletronic computer.

Home of the Philadelphia Cheesesteak

Yes, we are talking about the Philadelphia cheesesteak in this article. Many Philadelphians have different opinions about which place makes the best Philly cheesesteak. A few places will be on every Philadelphians list: Max's Steaks, Pat's King of Steaks, Geno's Steaks, Tony Luke's, Delassandro's, and Chubby's Steaks. The Singing Candy Lady

If you have ever walked the Center City streets of Philadelphia you are bound to encounter the Singing Candy Lady. She always happy to sell you a bag of m&m's and sing you a song while doing it. If you see her, be sure to say hi and support. She is a true Philly legend.
